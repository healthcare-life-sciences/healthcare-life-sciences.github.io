Revolutionizing Healthcare in Europe: Unleashing the Power of Knowledge Graphs for Personal Health Data Vaults

In this keynote, Hans Constandt will delve into the developments reshaping the healthcare landscape in Europe, with a focus on health data initiatives such as the European Health Data Space (EHDS), fostering patient empowerment and a new era of primary and secondary use of health data, the establishment of the Belgian Health Data Agency (HDA), and the transformative personal health data vault project in Flanders using Solid technology.


Hans will explain the potential paradigm shift in healthcare, placing the individual at the center through the FAQIR initiative. This novel approach builds a knowledge graph with, for, and from the patient's perspective, challenging traditional notions of data ownership and sharing, and pushing on data portability, data quality and FAIRness.


Crucial questions emerge: Who owns the data? Are individuals willing to share it? Studies reveal a resounding 85-95% willingness to share data when executed correctly, transparently, and securely. Governance, ethics and trust emerge as paramount considerations, while the decoupling of data from services becomes imperative.


The keynote unfolds the vision of a future where a big part of around 747 million Europeans possess their personal health data vault, with a majority open to safe sharing and data altruism. The exploration of the Solid principles and technology as a foundation of a decentralized data vault ecosystem reveals both promise and challenges, envisioning a continent connected through an interoperable semantic layer and knowledge graphs, fostering federated analytics without compromising privacy.


Driven by the passion to help patients through data, Hans will elucidate real-world examples illustrating the benefits for individuals, healthcare professionals, hospitals, researchers, and policymakers.


In conclusion, when the above is executed correctly, this revolutionary approach to health data management will not only save lives but also yield a substantial return on investment, fundamentally altering the healthcare landscape in Europe, and beyond.

Hans Constandt

Hans Constandt is a thought leader who demonstrates proficiency in scaling innovative projects in software engineering and health data management. As the Founding CEO of FAQIR, he is on a mission to empower patients to securely manage, control, understand, and share their health data, all while respecting their privacy and building trust. Additionally, as a mentor and investor, Hans actively supports and guides startups. With roles at Eli Lilly, ONTOFORCE, MedEx.Health, and startups, he contributes expertise and holds software patents, serving not only as a board member but also as an active executive. As a former Program Manager at the Belgian Health Data Agency, he played a pivotal role in shaping health data legislation and establishing the Belgian Health Data Catalog. Actively sharing insights globally, Hans is deeply committed to advancing data science and healthcare, with a strong belief in the #DataSavesLives mission.
